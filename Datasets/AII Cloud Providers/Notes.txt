SOURCE: Ministry of Foreign Affairs The People’s Republic of China

LINK: https://www.fmprc.gov.cn/eng/zwjg/zgdsg/2491_665346/

PERIOD: Current embassy presence

DESCRIPTION: A set of CSV files listing the locations of Chinese embassies across six continents, which are later combined into one dataset. A "Continent" column is added for geographic analysis. This reflects China's diplomatic footprint and global presence.

USE CASE: Analyze China’s diplomatic expansion, compare it with financial or cultural influence, or visualize global embassy distribution.
